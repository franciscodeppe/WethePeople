About

We go the extra mile beyond any other document preparation or filing service in the State of California.

We not only prepare documents for you, we also prepare them for filing. We The People offers a service that stands in line for you at the court and files your documents on your behalf. This feature saves our customers valuable time. Most document preparation services prepare the documents for you, give them to you and leave it up to you to do all the filing. This leads to a lot of guesswork and uncertainty.

At We The People, we pride ourselves on being a complete customer fulfillment service center. We guide you every step of the way.

We respect your time and privacy. Our commitment to customer service has put us where we are today, as one of the leading Document Preparation Services in Southern California.
